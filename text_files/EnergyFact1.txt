Energy consumption is a major source of global CO2 emissions, with the energy sector accounting for about 73% of global CO2 emissions in 2021.
Coal-fired power plants emit about 820 grams of CO2 per kWh of electricity generated.
Natural gas power plants emit about 450 grams of CO2 per kWh of electricity generated.
Wind and Solar have near-zero CO2 emissions during operation, though the production of equipment does have some emissions.
